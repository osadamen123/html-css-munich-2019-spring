OBA

Kampala is the national and commercial capital of Uganda. It borders Lake Victoria, the largest lake in Africa. The city center with its modern skyscrapers is surrounded by trees and hills with red brick villas. In the central Uganda Museum, tribal cultures are documented on the basis of extensive artefacts. Nearby Mengo Hill is home to the Lubiri Palace, former seat of the Buganda kingdom.

FAV

Nairobi is the capital of Kenya. In addition to the city center is located in the city of Nairobi National Park, a large game reserve in which the endangered black rhinos are bred. In addition, giraffes, zebras and lions live here. Next to it is a renowned elephant breeding facility run by the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust. Nairobi is often the starting point for safaris to other parts of Kenya.

London

London, the capital of England and Britain, is a modern city whose roots date back to the time of the Roman Empire. At its center are the imposing Houses of Parliament, the legendary clock tower "Big Ben" and Westminster Abbey, the coronation site of the British monarchs. On the other side of the Thames is the London Eye Ferris Wheel , which offers panoramic views of the South Bank cultural district and the rest of the city.
